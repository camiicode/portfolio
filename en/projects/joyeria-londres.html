Overview

This project began six years ago with a clear purpose: to develop Joyería Londres's digital presence through an online store within the Odoo ecosystem. At that time, I implemented the first version of their website in Odoo 11, adapting the design to the trends of that era. Over the years, I have been responsible for updating both the design and functionalities, keeping it at the forefront with significant improvements in interactivity and usability, ensuring an optimized experience for both desktop and mobile.

My Role

Designer and Web Developer.

  • Design and development of the user interface (UI): I used XML and XHTML in conjunction with SCSS to build a modern and responsive interface that reflects the brand's essence of luxury and exclusivity.
  • Interactive functionalities: I implemented interactive and customized elements using ECMAScript6, enhancing navigation and the online shopping experience.
  • Technological update: I migrated the website to a more advanced version of Odoo, version 13, optimizing the platform's performance and capabilities.
  • Mobile device adaptation: I worked on responsive design to ensure that the online store functioned smoothly on tablets and smartphones, expanding the reach to more customers.
  • Constant collaboration: I coordinated with the marketing and sales team to reflect the brand's branding strategies within the website.

Additionally, I have implemented additional modules to manage inventories, orders, and reports, effectively integrating them with Odoo's ERP system.

Impact

The work done on Joyería Londres's website has had a significant impact:

  1. Sales growth: The implementation of the online store allowed the company to reach a broader audience, increasing sales nationally and internationally.
  2. Brand strengthening: The site's design reflects values of exclusivity and luxury, which has improved the brand's perception in the digital market.
  3. Improvements in operational efficiency: The integration of Odoo with the website optimized internal processes, such as inventory management and customer service.
  4. Customer loyalty: The enhanced user experience and responsive design have generated a more loyal and satisfied customer base.
